Why Choose Us

Meet the Dentist

Dr. Gabriel Strauss

Dr. Gabriel Strauss, a board certified periodontist, is committed to providing the highest quality treatment. With a multitude of techniques in his repertoire, Dr. Strauss provides his patients with compassionate, safe and predictable results.

In addition to private practice, Dr. Strauss is involved in cutting-edge research including peri-implantitis, laser therapy, and orthodontic-periodontic interactions. His research has been published in the elite international dental journals. Dr. Strauss presents nationally on various topics related to periodontics and implant dentistry.

Our Services

Periodontal Procedures

Dr. Strauss provides a wide range of dental services from dental emergencies to periodontal cleanings, root planing and scaling, extractions, dental implants, gum grafts, and other specialty procedures relating to the gums. We use the most advanced non-surgical and surgical techniques and technology to treat you and restore your periodontal health to prevent the development of future periodontal problems.

Patient
Information

New Patients: If you're new to Periodontal Associates you can complete the new patient intake forms before you visit us.

Print and fill out all new patient forms. If you're unable to print and complete these forms, please arrive 10 minutes early for your scheduled appoinment to complete theme in office. Request an appoinment with us online or call our office! A front desk associate will get back to you as soon as possible to ensure proper schedulling. Upon arrival, please bring your complete forms, dental insurance card, Photo ID and referrals or x-rays your dentist has given you.
